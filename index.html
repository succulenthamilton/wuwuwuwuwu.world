About

Suzanne Shuran Wu is a graphic and object designer and maker. She recently graduated in Furniture Design from RISD though her interests have turned less fibrous. interested in accessibility, self-education, DIY toolmaking/hacking and storytelling.
In her senior year, she became intrigued in the possibility of self-help. Her thesis considers the effect our external environment's permanency has on our perception of our own powers. Developed her final thesis is an attempt at self-help through taking control of our living environments and creating a homemade system through personal ritual.
She continued her investigation on self-help after graduation, looking more broadly at the timeline and packaging of the concept, and different cultural perceptions on self-improvement.

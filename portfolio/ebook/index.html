MIT Press Ebooks

The MIT Press is the only nonprofit university press with a focus on science and technology.

I work with the book from its manuscript form to code it to its final .epub and .mobi version, making many decisions about the book design as it is interpreted on the different eBook readers. Some texts I've worked on includes A Natural History of Natural Theology by Helen DeCruz, Dream Chasers by John Tirman and Daedalus, an art and science journal.

With MIT Press, I'm also working with their CogNet website team to test the usability of their new website redesign on Drupal. I mainly ran searches by the search engine to see if it generated the most relevant results for all the different CogNet subscriber demographics from curious undergraduates to Ph.D researchers. I found some notable results: a bug that showed redundant results due to an overlapping node alias, and also what combinations of keywords were the most effective in creating effective results.

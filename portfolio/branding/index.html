ASPIRE website redesign

ASPIRE is a Boston non-profit aimed at helping Asian-American girls get more eduational opportunities, become more comfortable with their own identities and embrace leadership roles. They run programs such as the Youth Leadership Program, a mentorship for high school girls, the Asian American Woman in Leadership Conference and ASPIRE @ Work, all creating a multi-generational support system from the high school level all the way up to young professionals.

As a marketing team and web design volunteer, I worked with the marketing team to redesign the ASPIRE website. In-progress.

INNOVEX monthly Newsletter

INNOVEX Newsletter is a monthly internal newsletter circulated around the office. At the time it was in major need of a redesign: it suffered from low-readership due to being an entirely digital newsletter and its design wasn't properly reflecting the company brand.
The first step was to give it a more cohesive, readable format. Then I cleaned up the color usage by making INNOVEX teal and the grey as the default background and text colors and limiting orange and green for emphasis. The variety of stock graphic assets was also limited to only photos or customized vectors.

In order to make this newsletter more relevant to INNOVEX, for each month, I created themed questionnaires based on a holiday or current event (ex: new year resolutions for January, biggest guilty pleasures for the March Biggest Loser competition etc.). The questionnaire was then handed-out to everyone from all the various departments, encouraging co-workers who would never have communicated with each other otherwise find out a little more about each other's likes and dislikes, dreamsee through these fun questionnaires.
